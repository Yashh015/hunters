What We Stand For

Not a directory.
An opinion.

Most platforms want more listings. We want fewer — only the ones that actually matter. Our editorial standards are the product.

01
Quality over quantity
10 places you'll love beats 100 you'll ignore. We'd rather list nothing than list mediocrity.
02
Honest. Always.
If a place is overrated, we say so. No diplomatic vagueness, no hedging. You trust us or you don't.
03
Zero paid placements
No venue has paid to appear here. The moment that changes, this stops being useful — and we know it.
04
Local expertise only
We're from Vadodara. We eat here, live here, go out here. This isn't research — it's lived experience.
